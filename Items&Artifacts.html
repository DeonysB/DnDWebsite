List of Items & Artifacts from DnD

Item Name Type Rarity Description Notes
Bag of Holding Wondrous Item Uncommon A magical bag that can hold far more than its exterior size suggests. Great for carrying large amounts of treasure.
Ring of Invisibility Ring Legendary Grants the wearer invisibility while worn and activated. Perfect for stealth missions.
Cloak of Displacement Wondrous Item Rare Causes attackers to have disadvantage on attack rolls against you. Excellent for dodging attacks.
Immovable Rod Rod Uncommon A rod that, when activated, becomes fixed in place and immovable. Useful for traps or holding doors shut.
Wand of Fireballs Wand Rare A wand capable of casting the Fireball spell multiple times. Great for area-of-effect damage.
Deck of Many Things Wondrous Item Legendary A deck of cards that produces random magical effects, both beneficial and disastrous. Highly chaotic and unpredictable.
Sword of Sharpness Weapon (Sword) Rare A sword that deals extra damage and can sever limbs on a critical hit. Deadly against tough enemies.
Broom of Flying Wondrous Item Uncommon A magical broom that allows the user to fly. Great for mobility and exploration.
Amulet of Health Wondrous Item Rare Increases the wearer's Constitution score to 19 while worn. Boosts health significantly.
Portable Hole Wondrous Item Rare Creates an extradimensional space for storage or transportation. Handy for storing bulky items.
Boots of Speed Wondrous Item Rare When activated, these boots double your walking speed and grant advantage on Dexterity saving throws. Great for quick escapes or chasing enemies.
Belt of Giant Strength Wondrous Item Rare to Legendary Increases your Strength score to match that of a specific giant type (e.g., Hill Giant, Storm Giant). Essential for melee fighters.
Helm of Telepathy Wondrous Item Uncommon Allows the wearer to communicate telepathically and cast Suggestion on a creature. Useful for mind control and information gathering.
Gauntlets of Ogre Power Wondrous Item Uncommon Set your Strength score to 19 while wearing these gauntlets. Great for characters with low Strength.
Ring of Protection Ring Rare Grants a +1 bonus to AC and saving throws while worn. Ideal for survivability.
Cloak of Elvenkind Wondrous Item Uncommon Grants advantage on Stealth checks while the hood is up. Perfect for sneaky characters.
Staff of the Magi Staff Legendary A powerful staff that grants spell slots, spell absorption, and numerous spellcasting options. One of the strongest items for spellcasters.
Bracers of Defense Wondrous Item Rare Grants a +2 bonus to AC if you are wearing no armor or shield. Great for monks and mages.
Amulet of the Planes Wondrous Item Very Rare Allows you to teleport yourself and others to a different plane of existence. Perfect for interplanar adventures.
Luckstone Wondrous Item Uncommon Grants a +1 bonus to ability checks and saving throws while carried. Small but consistently useful.
Ring of Spell Storing Ring Rare Stores spells cast into it, which can then be used by the wearer. Perfect for preparing extra spells.
Boots of Levitation Wondrous Item Rare Allow the wearer to levitate up or down at will. Useful for vertical movement or avoiding traps.
Cape of the Mountebank Wondrous Item Rare Lets you cast Dimension Door once per day. Great for quick escapes or repositioning.
Horn of Blasting Wondrous Item Rare Emits a thunderous blast that damages creatures and objects in its area. Excellent for breaking obstacles.
Deck of Illusions Wondrous Item Uncommon Draw a card to create a random illusory creature. Fun and unpredictable utility item.
Instrument of the Bards Wondrous Item Varies A magical instrument that enhances bardic spellcasting and abilities. Essential for bard players.
Staff of Power Staff Very Rare A versatile staff granting bonuses to AC, spell attacks, and saving throws. Highly valuable for spellcasters.
Manual of Gainful Exercise Wondrous Item Very Rare Increases your Strength permanently after studying it. Great for physical characters.
Manual of Quickness of Action Wondrous Item Very Rare Increases your Dexterity permanently after studying it. Perfect for rogues and dexterity-based fighters.
Winged Boots Wondrous Item Uncommon Grant the ability to fly for short periods. Excellent mobility for ground-based characters.
Cubic Gate Wondrous Item Legendary A cube with six sides, each linked to a different plane of existence. Great for interplanar travel or summoning.
Robe of Stars Wondrous Item Very Rare A robe adorned with stars that allows you to cast Magic Missile and travel to the Astral Plane. Stylish and powerful for casters.
Gloves of Thievery Wondrous Item Uncommon Invisible gloves that grant a bonus to Sleight of Hand and lockpicking. Perfect for rogues.
Rod of Lordly Might Rod Legendary A rod with multiple transformative options, including a weapon, ladder, and more. Highly versatile tool and weapon.
Ring of Regeneration Ring Very Rare Grants the wearer healing over time and regenerates lost body parts. Excellent for long-term survivability.
Figurine of Wondrous Power Wondrous Item Varies A small statue that transforms into a magical creature to aid its owner. Variety of options for utility or combat.
Periapt of Wound Closure Wondrous Item Uncommon Stabilizes you when you are dying and doubles hit points restored from healing. Invaluable for frontline fighters.
Ring of Resistance Ring Rare Grants resistance to a specific damage type. Choose the type based on your campaign needs.
Sphere of Annihilation Wondrous Item Legendary A black sphere that destroys everything it touches. Highly dangerous and destructive.
Talisman of Ultimate Evil Wondrous Item Legendary A talisman that can destroy good-aligned characters and bolster evil ones. Powerful but alignment-dependent.
Staff of the Woodlands Staff Rare A staff that allows you to cast various druidic spells and create a wall of thorns. Ideal for druids.
Amulet of Mighty Fists Wondrous Item Rare Grants bonus to unarmed strikes and natural attacks. Perfect for monks and beast-shaped druids.
Rod of Absorption Rod Very Rare Absorbs incoming spells and converts them into spell slots. Extremely useful for spellcasters.
Helm of Brilliance Wondrous Item Very Rare A jeweled helm that allows you to cast spells and resist fire damage. Great for casters in fiery environments.
Orb of Dragonkind Wondrous Item Legendary A powerful orb that can summon dragons and control them. Highly situational but extremely powerful.
Horn of Valhalla Wondrous Item Varies Summons spectral warriors to fight for you. Perfect for overwhelming enemies.
Rod of Security Rod Legendary Creates a safe extradimensional space for rest and recovery. Great for escaping dangerous situations.
Dragon Slayer Weapon (Sword) Rare A weapon that deals extra damage to dragons. Essential for campaigns with dragons.
Efreeti Bottle Wondrous Item Very Rare A magical bottle that can summon an efreeti to grant wishes or fight for you. Highly unpredictable but powerful.
Vorpal Sword Weapon (Sword) Legendary A blade that can decapitate foes on a critical hit. One of the most iconic and deadly weapons.
