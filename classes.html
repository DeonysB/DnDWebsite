Barbarian

Barbarians are primal warriors who tap into their unbridled fury to gain incredible strength, resilience, and combat prowess. Fueled by rage, they can withstand blows that would fell lesser warriors and deliver devastating attacks with raw power. Their connection to primal spirits or ancestral lines often shapes their rage into something supernatural, granting them abilities like enhanced senses or resistance to magic. They are ideal for frontline combat, charging headfirst into battle with unmatched ferocity.
